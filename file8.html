Ingoma Yanjye (My Kingdom)

nspired by Rwanda’s regal fashion, Moshions presents a collection that speaks class, elegance, and grace of ancient kings and queens. An eclectic movement to pay tribute to the game-changing monochromatic and geometric designs, imigongo, discovered in the 18th century. The royal sensation makes its way into the contemporary realm; exploring the unmatched beauty of Rwanda’s kingdom fashion.

Dream Team: Moshions x Ifele

Moshions has collaborated with one of Africa’s best designers to make this collection the best yet. Ifele, South Africa’s very own, is an iconic sandals brand in that handcrafts leather sandals that hint at the continent’s rich culture, and adorned with modern motifs. The geometric elements from both brands align and correspond to breathe out proportions of timeless fashion. Ifele is about heritage and contemporary class. Just like Moshions. his collection is a time machine to unsung heritage, mirrored into the future for a timeless cultural embrace. Laid-back, majestic and dreamy in every pattern. Ingoma Yanjye revisits Africa’s heritage and silhouettes of heritage reimagined for an exotic appeal. Every piece evokes noble simplicity. Discover more about Ifele at ifele.com

Intsinzi Collection

The beating drums, triumphant shouts, and joyful faces represent victory. The amalgam of such sounds carry celebrations. Intsinzi Collection is a brand new way to celebrate; where victory meets fashion. It reflects strength, self-worth and avid ambition for success. This collection is also a reminder of how victory is part of us Africans, makes us, and knits us together as a flourishing community. Beautifully designed in Rwanda, Intsinzi is a gift from us to the world. Every piece in the collection is bold, fierce, and flattering. It recognizes significant warriors who defied impossibilities and inspire us to move forward every day. Victory is yours and mine – let’s rock it in style!
